Cricket Players of England

Joe Root

Joe Root is a right handed batsman who plays for england and scored over 12500 runs in test CricketRoot made his Test debut in 2012, his ODI debut in 2013, and played for the England Twenty20 International team between 2012 and 2019. He captained England's Test team between February 2017 and April 2022,[4] and holds the records for most Test matches (64), wins (27) and losses (26) as England captain.[5] On the occasion of England's 1,000th Test in 2018, Root was named in the country's greatest all-time Test XI by the England and Wales Cricket Board.[6] He was named both the ICC Men's Test Cricketer of the Year and the Wisden Leading Cricketer in the World for 2021.[7] .

Buttler

Buttler is a Captain of England cricket team who won t20 mens worldcup for England in 2022Buttler plays as a right-handed wicket-keeper-batsman. He is England's second-most-capped T20I player behind Eoin Morgan.[4] Along with Adil Rashid, he holds the world record for highest seventh-wicket stand in ODIs: 177 against New Zealand during their 2015 tour of England.[5] He is England's highest run-scorer in T20Is,[6] and is one of only five Englishmen to score a T20I century.[7] As a wicket-keeper, he holds the England record for most dismissals in both ODI and T20I formats.[8][9] .
